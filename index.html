Mischief Manager

Track your triggers, build mindful habits, and break free from add to cart overspending with our intuitive app designed to help you regain control of your spending.

Features

  • Track Emotional & Environmental Triggers: Identify and track emotional and environmental factors that influence your spending habits.
  • Customizable Goal Setting & Habit Tracking: Set specific goals to reduce impulsive spending and track your progress over time.
  • Mindful Spending Reminders: Receive gentle nudges before making a purchase, prompting you to reflect on your goals.
  • Impulse Awareness Insights: Personalized insights into your spending habits, with tips to improve self-control.
  • Community Support & Accountability: Connect with others to share tips, stay motivated, and build a supportive community.
  • Behavioral Analytics Dashboard: View detailed analytics of your spending patterns and progress towards your goals.
  • Alternatives to Impulsive Actions: Get suggestions for healthier activities or “side quests” to refocus your energy.
  • Personalized Behavioral Feedback: Regular feedback on your behavior and tips for continued improvement.
